Collaborations

Work made alongside.

Places, brands and experiences the studio has studied, documented and worked with.

mkd STUDIO creates editorial publications for places, brands and experiences with a story. Photography is the medium. The publication is the outcome.

The work may begin as an independent study, an invited visit or a commissioned publication, but the intention remains the same: to document atmosphere, identity and the feeling of a place with care. The scope varies. The approach does not.

Hotels. Destinations. Founder-led businesses. Cultural institutions. Sporting experiences.
