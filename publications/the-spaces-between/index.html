Publication Statement

The Spaces Between is a photographic publication exploring Formula One away from the racing line.

Across Silverstone and Lusail, attention shifts from competition to atmosphere, observing the architecture, movement, labour, hospitality and ritual that exist around the event itself.

What emerges is not a study of racing, but of the spaces that make racing possible.
