Editor's Note

mkd STUDIO creates commissioned editorial publications for places, brands and experiences with a story.

Photography is the medium. The publication is the outcome.

Each publication combines photography, writing and design to document atmosphere, identity and the feeling of a place.

We are interested in the moments that shape an experience.

The light in a hotel lobby before breakfast service. The quiet confidence of a well-made fragrance. The anticipation before the lights go out at a Grand Prix.

The moments that shape an experience are often the ones that never appear in a brochure.

This studio exists to document them.

About the Studio

mkd STUDIO is an editorial practice documenting places, objects and experiences through photography and film. The archive grows through observation rather than assignment, with new studies added as they are made. Selected work is also developed into long-form publications, complete editorial volumes built for a single place, brand or world and produced as both digital readers and printed first-edition books.

The same approach is available to selected hospitality, fragrance, travel and cultural partners seeking considered visual storytelling.
