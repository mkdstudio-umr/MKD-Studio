Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They can be used to remember your preferences, measure how a site is used, or track your behaviour across multiple sites for advertising purposes.

Cookies we set

We do not set any first-party cookies for analytics, tracking or advertising. mkdstudio.net does not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or any equivalent service.

Third-party cookies

This site loads typefaces from Google Fonts (fonts.googleapis.com). When your browser requests these fonts, a connection is made to Google's servers. Google may store cookies or log data including your IP address as part of this request. This is the only third-party service our site calls.

We do not have control over the cookies or data Google Fonts may set. Google's Cookie Policy applies to that interaction.

Why we do not show a cookie banner

UK law requires consent for non-essential cookies. Because this site does not use analytics, advertising or behavioural tracking cookies, a consent banner is not required. The only third-party connection we make is for web fonts, which falls within the technical operation of the site.

Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete stored cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all third-party cookies

Blocking the Google Fonts connection may cause typefaces to fall back to your system defaults. The site will remain fully usable.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to hello@mkdstudio.net. For broader privacy matters, see our Privacy Policy.
