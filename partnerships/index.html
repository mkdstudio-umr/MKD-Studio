Partnerships

Trusted across worlds.

A selection of the places, brands and experiences the studio has worked with.

mkd STUDIO creates editorial publications for organisations whose work is better experienced than explained. Through photography, writing and design, each commission produces something a brochure cannot: a document of what a place or brand actually feels like, made with a point of view.

The outcome is always a publication or a body of photography built to last. Some commissions are complete publications, with photography, writing and design working together across a sustained body of work. Others are narrower: a single event documented with an editorial eye, a hotel captured before it opens, a fragrance house photographed through its spaces rather than its products. The scope varies. The approach does not.

Hotels. Destinations. Founder-led businesses. Cultural institutions. Sporting experiences.
