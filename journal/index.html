Journal

The archive records what was seen.
The Journal reflects on why it mattered.

Some observations become photographs. Others become essays. The Journal gathers the ideas that sit behind the archive: reflections on hospitality, place, craft, publishing and the quiet details that shape how somewhere is remembered. The photographs remain the evidence. The writing asks the reader to look again.
