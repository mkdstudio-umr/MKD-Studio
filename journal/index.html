Journal

Why it mattered.

The archive records what was seen. The Journal reflects on why it mattered. Some observations become photographs. Others take longer to settle, and become essays instead. The Journal gathers reflections on hospitality, place, craft, publishing and the practice of looking closely at what is already there.
