FUEGUIA 1833, Milan

A collection built slowly.
Setting

Some collections are built to be displayed. Others are built to be explored. Inside FUEGUIA 1833, shelves rise towards the ceiling and ladders wait beside them. The space feels less like a boutique and more like an archive still in the process of being assembled.

Archive

The house draws together art, botany and memory. The fragrances matter, but the archive around them matters just as much.

Collection

What remains is a feeling of curiosity. The sense that there is always another shelf to discover.

Response

The object is only part of the story.
