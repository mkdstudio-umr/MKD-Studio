Porto, after the river

Porto, Portugal
Water

Porto appears through movement.

A boat held below the city wall. A tram crossing in evening light. The city does not announce itself — it gathers through water, glass and the slow passage of people between them.

Interior

Inside the stations and shopfronts, the light changes register.

Stained glass above a staircase. A counter set before the morning order. The carved spiral of a bookshop rising toward a coloured ceiling. These interiors carry more weight than the view from outside them.

River

The Douro receives the last of the sun.

At the waterfront, the city lowers itself toward the water. Tracks cross the bridge in both directions. A figure waits beside the tram. The colour holds briefly, then the sky clears.
