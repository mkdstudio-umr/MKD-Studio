Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Who we are

mkd STUDIO is an editorial studio based in West Yorkshire, United Kingdom. We create commissioned editorial publications for places, brands and experiences.

For any questions regarding this policy or your personal data, contact us at hello@mkdstudio.net.

Data we collect

We do not operate a contact form. We do not collect or store personal data automatically through this website.

If you choose to contact us by email, we will receive your email address and the contents of your message. This information is used solely to respond to your enquiry. We do not share it with third parties and we do not use it for marketing purposes.

Email correspondence is retained for as long as it remains relevant to the enquiry or any ongoing relationship. You may request deletion at any time.

Website analytics

We do not use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or any other analytics or tracking service. We do not monitor individual visitor behaviour on this website.

Third-party services

This website loads typefaces (Cormorant Garamond and Jost) directly from Google Fonts, a service operated by Google LLC. When your browser requests these fonts, your IP address and browser information are transmitted to Google's servers. We use this service to ensure consistent typography across devices.

Google's handling of this data is governed by Google's own privacy policy. We do not receive or process this data ourselves.

Cookies

We do not set tracking, analytics or advertising cookies. For full details of what cookies this site uses, please see our Cookie Policy.

Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict processing of your data
  • Request a portable copy of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@mkdstudio.net. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects when it was last revised. Continued use of the site following any update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
